Identity Verification (KYC)

Steps to Complete Your KYC:

  1. Step 1: Enter your PAN card details.
  2. Step 2: Upload a clear image of your PAN card.
  3. Step 3: Confirm all information before submitting.
  4. Step 4: Submit the form to begin the verification process.
  5. Step 5: Wait for verification to access the loan prediction dashboard.

* Image size should be 880 x 495.
