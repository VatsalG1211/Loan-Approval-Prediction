Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Loan Approval Prediction platform work?

The platform uses AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze various parameters like credit score, income, employment history, and other relevant factors to predict the likelihood of loan approval. It evaluates these factors in real time and provides an instant decision based on the data.

What is Credit Score ?

CIBIL Score or Credit Score is a 3-digit numeric summary of your credit history, rating and report, and ranges from 300 to 900. The closer your score is to 900, the better your credit rating is. Check your CIBIL score

What id Debt-to-Income Ratio ?

Your debt-to-income ratio (DTI) is all your monthly debt payments divided by your gross monthly income. This number is one way lenders measure your ability to manage the monthly payments to repay the money you plan to borrow.Calculate debt-to-income ratio

Is my data secure on the Loan Approval Prediction platform?

Yes, your data is fully secured. We use advanced encryption and secure authentication measures to ensure that all personal and financial information remains confidential and protected from unauthorized access.

Can I use the platform if I have no prior knowledge of loan processes?

Absolutely! The platform is designed to be user-friendly and requires no prior knowledge of loan processes. Simply enter your details, and the platform will guide you through the rest.

What kind of loans does the platform support for prediction?

The platform supports a wide range of loans, including personal loans, home loans, car loans, and business loans. Our models are customized to analyze the specific criteria relevant to each loan type.
